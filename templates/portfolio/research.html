UVA Solar Car Team

During my first year of university, I joined the UVA solar car team.
Solar car was a great experience, I was able to acquire a variety of
different skills such as 3D design and welding. I eventually
became the steering geometry lead of the mechanical sector and
through extensive research of steering geometry, my team was able
to produce a finished product for the car. That summer I had the privilege
to attend the FSGP summer race where I was exposed to various different
levels of design and engineering.

Charlottesville Ecovillage

During Spring 2019, I joined an organization at UVA called Engineers
Going Global where students could participate in various research projects.
My team was tasked to work with the local Ecovillage in Charlottesville,
Virginia. The Ecovillage aims to create a community that lives sustainably.
Our task was to design and build a playground piece that was not only
sustainable, but also transportable. During this project, I learned how to
pitch engineering projects and acquire grants while also further developing
my engineering skills in a team environment.
